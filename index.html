Tribute to Jafet Ramos

Mexican Surfing Prodigy

Charging at Zicatela, Puerto Escondido. Photo by Omar Diaz.

Jafet Ramos is a 19-year-old surfer who lives and rides in Puerto Escondido, Oaxaca, Mexico. He started surfing when he was 13 and is in the water every day. He's a hard worker. And he is a gifted rider.

  • 2012 - Winner - Junior Competition - Carrizalillo, Puerto Escondido.
  • 2014 - 2nd Place - Quicksilver Pro Competition - Zicatela, Puerto Escondido.
  • 2014 - 1st Place - Coco Nogales Open Competition - Zicatela, Puerto Escondido.
  • 2015 - 2nd Place - Rip Curl Competition - Acapulco. Mexico
  • 2016 - 1st Place - WSL International Competition - Baja California, Mexico

Jafet has won many competitions and has had some success securing sponsorships. Unfortunately, systemic support is spotty, at best. Getting boards and clothes doesn't get him to national and, even less, international events.

This blazing talent is passing the age of transition to the world scene. He has the misfortune of being born with little support at every level. His friends and mentors do what they can to help, but it's not enough.
